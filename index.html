My name is Kristine and I graduated from Middle Tennessee State University in 2019. I started out pursuing an Associate's Degree in Fine Art at Roane State Community College, and I later transferred to MTSU. Since then, have had experiences in designing identities, stationary, packaging, posters, an album cover, campaigns, magazine spreads, and franchise kits. I've also done some work with web design and some work outside school.

Throughout my experiences in art and design, I have found interest in learning more about how I can make something beautiful. I have seen some great works on sources like Pinterest which have given me an aspiration to challenge myself to become a better artist.
